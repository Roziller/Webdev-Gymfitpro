Challenge Name

Strength Normal No Equipment

About This Challenge

This 30-day challenge is designed to help you build strength, endurance, and confidence. Each day presents a new set of exercises that progressively increase in intensity to help you achieve your fitness goals.

Duration: 30 days
Time per day: 15-30 minutes
Equipment: None required
Calories: ~150-300 per session

What You'll Achieve

  • Increased core strength and stability
  • Improved muscle tone and definition
  • Enhanced endurance and stamina
  • Better posture and body alignment
  • Boosted confidence and mental resilience

How to Use This Challenge

1

Follow the Schedule

Complete each day's workout as shown in the calendar below. Try to do them in order, but if you miss a day, just continue from where you left off.

2

Track Your Progress

Mark completed days to stay motivated and see your journey. Take before and after photos to visualize your transformation.

3

Listen to Your Body

Modify exercises as needed. If something feels too difficult, it's okay to take a break or adjust the intensity.

4

Stay Consistent

Consistency is key to seeing results. Try to stick with the challenge even on days when motivation is low.

Day 1 Exercises

Squats

How to perform:
  1. Stand with feet shoulder-width apart
  2. Lower your body as if sitting in a chair
  3. Keep your chest up and knees tracking over toes
  4. Push through heels to return to standing
Tips:
  • Keep your weight in your heels
  • Don't let your knees go past your toes
  • Engage your core throughout the movement
